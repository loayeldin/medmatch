prescription scan

A prescription scanning system is a technology-based solution designed to automate the process of scanning and extracting information from prescription documents. This system utilizes optical character recognition (OCR) and image processing techniques to convert physical prescriptions into digital, machine-readable format.

The benefits of a prescription scanning system include increased efficiency, reduced manual errors in data entry, and improved accuracy in medication management. Healthcare professionals can easily access and store digital prescriptions, making it easier to review and manage patient information.

You have the option to upload an image for scanning or to capture a picture of the prescription

drug details

Drug details encompass a wide range of information, including the drug's name, active ingredients and price. These details enable healthcare providers to make accurate prescribing decisions tailored to individual patient needs and medical histories. Additionally, they equip patients with the knowledge necessary to actively participate in their treatment plans and ask informed questions about their medications.

similar drugs

Similar drugs are medications that have the same active ingredient . They share common mechanisms of action and are used to treat the same conditions or address similar symptoms. However, slight variations in formulation, dosage, or side effect profiles may make one drug more suitable for a specific patient compared to another.
